Basic Overview of the Elaina Mod for Terraria (TModLoader) SOURCE

Character Creation & Buffs

  • Selecting Elaina’s icon halves max HP gain, prevents magic disease, and auto-uses a mana potion.
  • Picking up money grants +10 mana and a “Happy” buff for faster regeneration.

Cauldron / Refining System

  • Right-click cauldron to start crafting; “?” shows recipes.
  • Refinable items:
    • Ultimate Mana Potion (fake, restores 30 mana, sellable)
    • Magic enhancement potions
    • Animal Potions (transform into small animals with custom actions)
    • Item Cloning (failure chance if rarity too high or repeated attempts)

Proficiency System

  • Increases only by defeating bosses on the map.
  • Affects magic power, broom performance, and cauldron refining success.

Magic Types

MagicMechanics
Magic MissileBasic projectile attack.
Magic ShieldRight-click wand to deploy, consumes mana, blocks projectiles/repels enemies.
IlluminationMiddle-click to toggle light, drains mana while active.
Healing MagicHeals status and HP over time. Alternate modes: Block Control (place blocks anywhere) or Weapon Control (convert weapons to magic type, control up to proficiency level).
Water MagicGather water with wand, consume or absorb mana. Applies “wet” debuff, can release stored water, full charge gives guaranteed crits.
Fire MagicFireballs cause burning; hold to beam fire. Can ignite terrain, evaporate water, and deal bonus vs. wet enemies.
Ice MagicIce fog to freeze water/enemies, ice spikes track targets, freezing synergy.
Wind MagicWind blades knock back enemies/items, chance of large blade to knock bosses, environment affects power.
Lightning MagicHold to summon storm strikes, click to chain lightning. Wet targets spread lightning.
